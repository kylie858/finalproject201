BYU Ballroom

Ballroom dancing is the best!

The Brigham Young University (BYU) Ballroom Dance Program is widely regarded as one of the best in the world. Known for its exceptional training, remarkable performance opportunities, and a legacy of producing top-tier dancers, the program has solidified its reputation as a powerhouse in the world of competitive ballroom dance. With its roots deeply embedded in the university's values of excellence, dedication, and service, the BYU Ballroom Program offers a unique blend of artistry and athleticism, attracting students from all over the globe. The program fosters a sense of community, discipline, and passion that permeates every aspect of its operations, creating an environment where students can truly thrive.

What is Taught?

A key feature of the BYU Ballroom Dance Program is its emphasis on both technical precision and expressive artistry. The program focuses on developing dancers' mastery of all ballroom dance styles, including Latin, Standard, and Smooth, while also encouraging creativity and personal expression. Students are trained by some of the most accomplished professionals in the field, many of whom have a history of competing at the highest levels of international dance. This dual focus on technique and performance artistry ensures that BYU dancers excel not only in competitions but also in their ability to communicate through movement and emotion.

BYU Competitions

One of the most impressive aspects of the BYU Ballroom Dance Program is its success in national and international competitions. The university has a long-standing tradition of winning prestigious titles, including multiple National DanceSport Championships. The program’s consistent success is a testament to the exceptional caliber of the dancers and the dedication of the instructors. Students who participate in the program have the opportunity to train at an elite level, competing against the best dancers in the world, and many of them go on to have successful careers in the professional dance industry.

Ballroom Productions

In addition to its competitive achievements, the BYU Ballroom Dance Program provides students with valuable performance opportunities that allow them to showcase their talents to a wide audience. The program regularly performs in large-scale productions, including the BYU Ballroom Dance Company’s annual performances and other campus events. These performances are not only a showcase of the dancers' technical skill but also an opportunity to engage with the community, sharing the joy and beauty of ballroom dance. For students, these experiences help develop stage presence and confidence, while also allowing them to reach new heights in their performance abilities.

Individual Growth

Beyond the technical and artistic components, the BYU Ballroom Dance Program places a strong emphasis on personal growth and character development. Students are encouraged to embrace values such as teamwork, discipline, and perseverance, all of which are essential to success both in dance and in life. The tight-knit nature of the program fosters lasting friendships and a sense of camaraderie among students, creating an environment where everyone supports one another. This supportive atmosphere is especially important for dancers who are learning to balance the demands of rigorous training with their academic responsibilities.

A Shining Example of Excellence

In conclusion, the BYU Ballroom Dance Program stands as a shining example of excellence in the world of competitive dance. Its combination of exceptional training, strong competitive results, invaluable performance opportunities, and emphasis on personal growth makes it a model for other programs around the world. The program not only produces technically skilled and artistic dancers but also shapes individuals who are poised for success in all aspects of life. For those fortunate enough to be part of it, the BYU Ballroom Dance Program offers an experience that is truly transformative.
